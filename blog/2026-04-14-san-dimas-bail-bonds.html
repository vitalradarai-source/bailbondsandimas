Frequently Asked Questions

Where does a San Dimas arrest actually get processed?

San Dimas is a LASD contract city. Deputies book arrestees at the San Dimas Sheriff's Station at 270 S Walnut Ave and transport to LA County IRC at 441 Bauchet Street. IRC processing is slow — 8–24 hours post-bond release is typical.

How much does a bail bond actually cost in San Dimas?

The bail bond fee is 10% of the total bail amount under California Insurance Code § 1800.4. For a first-offense DUI with bail set at $15,000, you pay $1,500. That 10% is our fee for posting the full bond — it is not refunded at case conclusion. Payment plans are available for qualifying clients.

Can San Dimas bail be posted the same night?

Yes, in most cases. California does not impose a mandatory overnight hold. Once the booking process is complete and the Los Angeles County bail schedule amount is assigned, a licensed bondsman can post the bond immediately. Most San Dimas DUI and misdemeanor arrests result in same-night or early-morning release.
